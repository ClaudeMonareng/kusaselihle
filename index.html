About Us

We hold a strong belief that a child's growth and development into adulthood is moulded and grounded in the foundation phase. Children enter the public schooling system ill-prepared, un-assessed of their true abilities and potential and lacking in foundational grounding, that is provided by creches and pre-schools. In solving these social-educational ills, Kusaselihle was founded.

Our founding pillars include but are not limited to the following:

To reduce child poverty in the rural community by ensuring that children receive at least two daily meals, in partnership with local stakeholders

To assist young mothers be able to continue and further their education.

To assist hardworking single parents achieve an important work-life balance

To assess and prepare our children to be physically and psychologically fit for the public schooling system
