About Us

We are a long time top creche of choice in our local area. We are recognized and accredited by the Department of Basic Education and entrusted by the local community with the custody, care and education of their children.

Our founding pillars include but are not limited to the following:

To reduce child poverty in the rural community by ensuring that children have at least two meals per day.

To introduce and maintain healthy food nutrition in the rural community.

To assist young mothers be able to continue and further their education.

To assist hardworking single parents achieve an important work-life balance

To do the above mentioned, while still ensuring that we continue to provide good quality education that equips our children for further education and future employment.
